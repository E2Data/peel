What is Peel?

Peel is a framework that helps you to define, execute, analyze, and share experiments for distributed systems and algorithms.

Why should I use it?

Check the Motivation section to understand the problems Peel will solve for you.

How to get started?

Check the Getting Started guide and the Bundle Basics section.

Define

Peel offers simple, DI-based experiment configuration.

Execute

Peel handles the entire experiment execution lifecycle.

Analyse

Peel extracts, transforms, and loads results into an RDBMS.

Share

Peel makes sharing your bundles with others painless.
